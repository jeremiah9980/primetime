The origin

Why Primetime Fastpitch Exists

Primetime Fastpitch is a Texas select softball organization competing across multiple age divisions. The organization is built on a full structural model — formal governance, defined roles, and a coaching staff that can focus on what they do best.

Most club programs are really just a single coach carrying everything — scheduling, finances, parent texts, hotel bookings, and lineups all running through one phone. That model produces burnout and inconsistency.

Primetime separates the org machine from the coaching machine. Co-Directors handle every organizational function. The coaching staff owns the field. A formal Board of Directors provides governance. The result is a program where everyone operates at full capacity in their actual role.

Core identity

Mission & Values

Mission: To develop elite young softball athletes through disciplined coaching, organized competition, and a team environment that builds confidence and love of the game — on and off the field.

Hard Work & Dedication

We don't look for shortcuts. Every rep, every practice, every game is an opportunity to get better. Primetime athletes are built on the commitment to show up and earn it every single day.

Discipline & Passion

Consistent, focused effort backed by genuine love for the game. Primetime athletes are coachable, accountable, and still love competing long after others have walked away.

Professional Operations

Formal board. Documented bylaws. Transparent finances. Clear communication channels. Families investing in this program deserve org standards that match their commitment.

Family First

Our coaching staff is family-first by design. The org is structured to support coaches being parents first — and that philosophy carries onto the field with every player.

The org structure

How It All Fits Together

Tier 1 — Executive
Co-Directors
All executive organizational authority — finance, scheduling, brand, communications, sponsorships, and org documents.
Tier 2 — Board of Directors
Governing Board — Three Seats
Three voting board members. Head Coach holds a seat representing the coaching staff.
Tier 3 — Head Coaching Staff
Head Coach + Assistant Staff
Lineups, development, strategy, rotations. Family-first coverage model. Zero administrative burden.
Tier 4 — Operations Staff
Team Mom · Scorekeeper · Travel Coordinator · Fundraising Coordinator
Appointed by Co-Directors. Game-day operations, GameChanger, travel logistics, and fundraising campaigns.
